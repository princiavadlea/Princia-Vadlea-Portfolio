About Me

I am a Senior Software Quality Assurance Tester and QA Consultant with over a decade of hands-on experience in ensuring software excellence across diverse industries and platforms.

My expertise spans the full testing spectrum — from crafting comprehensive test strategies and executing rigorous manual testing to building robust automation frameworks using Selenium, designing API test suites with Postman and REST Assured, and performing deep performance analysis with JMeter.

I am an ISTQB® Certified and Certified Software Tester (CSTE), and I bring a quality-first mindset to every project. Whether it's mobile app testing on Android, iOS, and iPad, or implementing CI/CD-integrated test pipelines, I am passionate about delivering zero-defect software products.

As a consultant, I partner with teams to streamline their QA processes, establish testing best practices, and build a culture of quality engineering that drives business confidence.
